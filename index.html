AppTimeOut

Lock your most distracting apps. Unlock them with help from someone you trust.

Purpose

AppTimeOut is a wellness and productivity app that helps users reduce digital distractions by locking selected apps and requiring accountability from a trusted partner.

This app uses Apple's FamilyControls and ScreenTime APIs solely to support intentional phone use and time-management.
