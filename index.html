AppTimeOut

Lock your most distracting apps. Unlock them with help from someone you trust.

Status: In development · iOS

--- ## What is AppTimeOut? **AppTimeOut** is a wellness and productivity app that helps users reduce digital distractions by locking selected apps and adding accountability from a trusted partner. Instead of relying on willpower alone, AppTimeOut lets you **commit to a lock** and optionally require a **partner-generated unlock code** before you can get back in. --- ## Key features (planned for v1) - **Quick Lock timer** Choose a lock duration (for example 30 minutes, 1 hour, or 3 hours) and instantly block your most distracting apps. - **Scheduled locks** Set recurring lock schedules (like weekday evenings) so your phone is quieter when you want to focus or relax. - **Partner-based unlock** Share an unlock code with a trusted partner so you can’t unlock early without talking to them first. - **Self-unlock option** Prefer to manage things on your own? You can keep the lock self-controlled while still using timers and schedules. --- ## Purpose This app uses Apple’s **FamilyControls** and **ScreenTime** APIs solely to support intentional phone use and time-management. AppTimeOut is designed for: - Students who want fewer distractions while studying - Parents who want help staying off social media - Anyone who wants a simple way to reduce screen time and improve focus --- ## Privacy Policy Your privacy is a core design principle. - **No screenshots or screen recordings** are collected - **No device usage logs** are uploaded to a server - **No sensitive personal information** (such as contacts, messages, or browsing history) is collected - All data stays on the user’s device unless the user explicitly chooses to share an unlock code with a partner If this privacy policy changes in the future, the app and this page will be updated before new data is collected. --- ## Developer & Contact **Developer:** Harry Huynh **Email:** harryhuynh.x@gmail.com If you’re interested in testing AppTimeOut when a beta is ready, feel free to reach out by email.
